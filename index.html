When and Where?

We will head to Soorts-Hossegor, a well-known city of south-western France hosting the ASP World Championship Tour's step Quicksilver Pro. It will be an awesome two week-long code / surf camp, starting on September 8th, 2014.

{% include map.html %}

For whom?

No pre-requisites on your surf level. Come as you are, we'll teach you how to surf and give you a board. If you are pro, that's great too!

Code Beginners

We will teach you front-end development (HTML, CSS, JavaScript) and back-end development (Ruby on Rails). When the swell is up and the tide right, we'll walk to the beach and score some waves!

Hackers

You are a full-stack developer, so you can skip the beginniner lessons. It's up to you to hack something cool with friends, or set up a small keynote about your best specialty. Learn & Share!

How much?

We will all stay in a big house with Wifi. Breakfast, lunch and dinner will be provided, boards and wetsuits for beginners. It's all-inclusive, so bring some clothes and your laptop, buy your plane or train ticket, we'll take care of the rest.

2500€ for code beginners

You will get top-notch education for 2 weeks!

1500€ for hackers

Again, all-inclusive.
